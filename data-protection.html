Remote Backup Services

With companies leveraging a mix of application and data storage arrangements both locally and in the cloud, protecting against data loss, theft, or damage has become more complex. L3’s remote backup service features a solution that centralizes client data in a highly secure facility that supports geographic diversity.

Your Data is a Priority

Organizations often realize the value and importance of a backup solution after data has already been lost and is unrecoverable. But backups should be viewed as a priority, rather than an item on your ‘to do’ list. L3 ensures data is backed up, verified, and stored safely in case you need to restore it.

Mitigate Risk

Deciding to put a back up solution in place is the first step. But can you be assured that your data will be safe? L3 offers the assurance of knowing that your data is backed up in a Private Environment, which is a more secure option than relying on a public cloud service that has less control and visibility.

Rapid Recovery

RL3 delivers a highly technical team with the tools and expertise to respond to data recovery requests. Ensure your business operations continue running with minimal impact if data has been lost or damaged.

A Single Technology Solution

Our Remote Backup Service leverages a single platform technology that supports both physical and virtual server environments, and is scalable in order to meet every organization’s backup needs.

Complete Visibility

Included with our single platform backup technology is a comprehensive toolset that features reporting, scheduling, and tracking capabilities. Having weekly and monthly reports gives clients full visibility of all backup related activities.

Disaster Recovery

Backing up your data is just one part of ensuring your business remains operational when unforeseen events happen. Having your infrastructure ‘staged’ and ready to go when needed is a vital insurance policy for when your primary system is lost. L3’s Disaster Recovery solutions provide an affordable and meaningful way to guarantee your company can still conduct business as usual when the primary infrastructure has been compromised.

Disaster Recovery ‘As a Service’

L3’s private cloud infrastructure and expertise in building and managing disaster recovery environments presents businesses with an affordable solution. Clients pay only for what they need, with an option to increase resources at the time of a disaster.

Flexible Infrastructure Options

Whether your Infrastructure is currently virtualized or not, L3 delivers flexible solutions that meet you specific business continuity requirements with cold or warm standby Infrastructure ready to go if your server is lost.

Fully Managed or Self Service

When you rely on L3 to manage your disaster recovery environment, your IT team is freed from the time consuming process of validating data replication on an ongoing basis. For CIOs that prefer to be in full control, L3 provides infrastructure for your team to manage.

RTO/RPO Assurance

By having clearly defined Recovery Time Objectives and Recovery Point Objectives, L3 provides clients peace of mind that their IT systems will be online in accordance with their business continuity plan even in the wake of a disaster.
