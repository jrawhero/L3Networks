Private Cloud

L3’s Private Cloud provides a robust, fully customizable data center environment for even the most demanding use case scenarios. Get on-demand computing, storage, network, and security services that can scale up or down as needed. From production to disaster recovery, you gain full control and deeper capabilities for advanced applications that simply can’t be accomplished in the public cloud arena. All underpinned with management services that are tailored to the specific needs of your business.

Why choose L3 Networks’ Private Cloud over the competition?

Virtual Private Cloud

Keep your upfront costs down by leveraging L3’s Virtual Cloud platform. Virtual Private Clouds combines the performance and scalability of a dedicated Private Cloud with the high availability and service level you would expect from an Enterprise environment.

L3 provides the resources and management interface to maintain your infrastructure with greater control and oversight.

Dedicated Private Cloud

L3’s Dedicated Private Cloud is a hybrid solution where you continue to use and manage dedicated server and storage equipment but gain access to our Private Cloud services for network infrastructure, security, data protection, and disaster recovery. L3 helps supplement your own cloud footprint with a unified set of management tools and services.

Benefit from a Collaborative Cloud Design

Consolidate your architecture and resource usage to improve IT service levels in your business. Partnering with L3 opens doors to a team that can help you make design, management and cost decisions, enabling cloud migration in a predictable way that helps deliver results on time and on budget.

Diverse Carrier Internet Access

L3 Network’s Private Cloud solutions utilize multi Gigabit diverse Internet connectivity from multiple datacenters. This eliminates single points of failure and ensures business service levels objectives are met 24/7.

Defend Against Security Threats

L3’s layered security architecture provides a comprehensive defense against malicious threats. Designed with flexibility in mind for our clients, L3 designs security models specifically tailored to individual clients rather than using a one size fits all approach.

Private WAN Connectivity

Available with both Virtual and Dedicated Private Cloud architectures, Private WAN Connectivity strengthens and secures multi-site networks, giving clients a higher level of flexibility in meeting production and disaster recovery requirements.

PCI DSS 3.0 Validated and Hardened Architecture

L3’s Private Cloud platforms are engineered to meet the high standards of PCI DSS 3.0 Compliance. Partnering with L3 helps clients satisfy attestation and maintain their security status.

Comprehensive Management & Reporting

L3 Networks’ secure management tools enable clients to be as ‘hands on’ as they wish to be. Or, businesses can simply entrust L3’s IT Management team to do all of the heavy lifting and provide strategic reporting to make key business decisions.

Determine Your Disaster Recovery Strategy

With our ability to provide Private Cloud infrastructure across multiple U.S. states, we become an obvious choice when considering a cost effective environment for either a self-managed Disaster Recovery or L3 Managed Disaster Recovery solution.

Only Pay for Service you Need

Leveraging L3’s Private Cloud options allows businesses to pay only for resources used and needed. Our ability to adopt this cost model to both the Dedicated and Virtual Cloud settings makes us uniquely placed to provide a truly flexible solution.

Get Better Backup and Restore Features

L3’s proactive support approach to IT management guarantees virtual server backup and restore capabilities to help clients recover quickly and easily from file corruption and data loss before business operations are affected.
